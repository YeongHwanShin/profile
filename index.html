Golden Capsule (2023)

Non-powered Medicine Injector for Disaster Situations

In disaster situations, emergency medical intervention is critical but often hampered by lack of power sources. Traditional injection systems require electricity or batteries, which are unreliable in crisis scenarios. Golden Capsule is a non-powered medicine injector that utilizes air pressure and elasticity to inject fluids in emergency situations.

The device leverages fundamental physics principles to create a reliable, fail-safe system that requires no batteries or electricity. By focusing on air pressure and material elasticity, we created a solution that works independently of external power infrastructure.

Role: Lead Designer, CAE Engineer, Researcher
Team: Yeonghwan Shin, Daeyeon Kim, Yujin Chae, Yuan Bai, Jiho Kim, Dokshin Lim
Tools: Fusion 360, Siemens NX, Ansys Discovery, Adams, Physical Prototyping

Recognition:

→ James Dyson Award International Winner (2023)
→ Prime Minister Prize at Capstone Design Fair (1st Place)
→ Creative Engineering Design Award at MMR Workshop

Minwon 99 (2024)

AI-powered Malicious Complaint Filtering

Civil servants in South Korea face an increasing volume of malicious complaints that consume valuable time and resources while creating emotional distress. Minwon 99 is an LLM-based text emotion moderation technology that automatically identifies and filters malicious civil complaints using advanced natural language processing.

The system analyzes emotional content, intent, and severity, helping civil servants prioritize legitimate concerns while protecting them from harmful content.

Team: Seong-min Choi, Hohyun Song, Yeonghwan Shin, Kyusung Lee, Eunji Park
Tools: Python, PyTorch, LLM APIs, NLP

Recognition:
→ Best Paper Prize at HCI Korea 2025 (Oral Presentation)

SophyBARA (2025)

Multi-Agent Philosophical Debate System

SophyBARA is a multi-agent system where AI agents embody different philosophers' thinking styles and engage in structured debates. Users can pose questions and observe philosophical discussions unfold, offering educational value and critical thinking training through exposure to diverse viewpoints.

The system uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) with historical philosophical texts and multi-agent orchestration to create authentic philosophical discourse.

Role: Research Lead, AI Developer
Team: Yeonghwan Shin, Wook-hee Won, Dohyun Kim, Yerim Lee, Sihyun Yoo, Minseo Yu, and team
Tools: n8n, GPT-4, RAG, Supabase, Multi-agent systems
Status: Active Research (2025)

Paw Pulse (2025)

AI Pet Health Monitoring with XR

Paw Pulse is an AI-powered pet health monitoring system that uses computer vision and behavioral analysis to track pets' wellbeing. The XR component provides immersive data visualization and early warning signs for potential health issues.

Features:
→ Computer Vision: Activity and behavior pattern recognition
→ Health Metrics: Movement, eating patterns, sleep quality tracking
→ Early Detection: AI algorithms identifying anomalies
→ XR Visualization: Immersive health data presentation

Recognition: XREAL XR Hackathon 2nd Winner (January 2025)
Tools: XREAL SDK, Unity, Python, Computer Vision, ML
